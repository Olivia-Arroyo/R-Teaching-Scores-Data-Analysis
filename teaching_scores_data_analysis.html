Data visualization: Scatterplot of the relationship between teaching and beauty scores

The positive slope of the regression line demonstrates a positive relationship, as instructors have a higher beauty score they also receive higher evaluations.

ggplot(evals_ch5, aes(x = bty_avg, y = score)) +
  geom_point() + 
  labs(x = "Beauty Score",
       y = "Teaching Score",
       title = "Scatterplot of relationship between teaching and beauty scores.") +
  geom_smooth(method = "lm", se = FALSE)
